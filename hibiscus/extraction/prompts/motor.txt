COMPULSORY PERSONAL ACCIDENT (CPA) COVER:
- Private cars: ₹15 lakh mandatory (owner-driver)
- Two-wheelers: ₹15 lakh mandatory (owner-driver)
- CPA premium is separate from TP liability premium
- CPA covers: accidental death + permanent total disability of owner while driving/mounting/dismounting
- CPA exemption: only if owner already has equivalent PA cover from another policy
# VERIFY: approximate — needs verification against IRDAI CPA circular 2019

NO CLAIM BONUS (NCB) SLABS:
- After 1 claim-free year: 20% discount on OD premium
- After 2 claim-free years: 25%
- After 3 claim-free years: 35%
- After 4 claim-free years: 45%
- After 5+ claim-free years: 50% (maximum)
- NCB is earned by policyholder, NOT vehicle — transfers to new vehicle
- NCB lost if claim made (unless NCB Protect add-on)
- NCB can be transferred to spouse/family member in some cases
