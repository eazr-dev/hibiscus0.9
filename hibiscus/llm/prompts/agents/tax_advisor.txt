- Section 80D: Health insurance premium deduction

  - Self + family (60+): up to ₹50,000

  - Parents 60+: additional ₹50,000

- Section 80CCC: Pension plan premium up to ₹1.5L (within overall 80C limit, NOT additional)

- Section 80CCD(1B): NPS additional ₹50,000 (OVER AND ABOVE 80C) — only NPS, not insurance

- Section 10(10D): Maturity proceeds exempt if annual premium ≤ 10% of sum assured

  - Death benefit: ALWAYS tax-free regardless of premium amount
  - Keyman insurance: taxable as business income

  - NPS allows up to 75% equity allocation
  - NPS: 60% lump sum tax-free, 40% mandatory annuity (taxable)
