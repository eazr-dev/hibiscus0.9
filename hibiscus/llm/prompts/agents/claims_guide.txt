EMBEDDED DOMAIN EXPERTISE:

STANDARD CLAIM TIMELINE (IRDAI mandated):
- Intimation to insurer: within 24 hours of hospitalization
- Document submission: within 15-30 days of discharge
- Insurer decision: within 30 days of receiving all documents
- Cashless pre-auth: 1 hour (emergency), 3 hours (planned) — IRDAI mandate
- Interest on delayed settlement: 2% above bank rate for delays beyond 30 days

CASHLESS CLAIM PROCESS (step-by-step):
1. Go to network hospital — verify on insurer's current network list
2. Show health card/policy number at hospital TPA desk
3. Hospital submits pre-auth form to insurer/TPA
4. Insurer approves/queries/rejects within 1 hour (emergency) or 3 hours (planned)
5. If approved: treatment proceeds, hospital bills insurer directly
6. Copay/deductible/non-covered items: patient pays at discharge
7. Final bill settlement between hospital and insurer within 7-10 days

REIMBURSEMENT CLAIM PROCESS:
1. Inform insurer within 24 hours (call + written intimation)
2. Get treated at any hospital
3. Pay bills — collect ALL originals (bills, discharge summary, prescriptions, investigation reports)
4. Submit claim form + documents within 15-30 days of discharge
5. Insurer processes within 30 days of receiving complete documents
6. Settlement via NEFT to registered bank account

COMMON REJECTION REASONS (and counters):
- Non-disclosure of PED (35%): Counter with moratorium rule (5yr) or insurer didn't do pre-policy medical
- Waiting period not met (25%): Verify exact dates, check portability credit
- Excluded treatment (20%): Check if exclusion is IRDAI standard or insurer-specific
- Document issues (15%): Re-submit, ask for specific list of missing items
- Policy lapsed (5%): Check grace period, revival options

ESCALATION PATH:
1. Insurer customer care → get complaint reference number
2. Insurer Grievance Officer → written complaint, 15-day SLA
3. IRDAI Bima Bharosa Portal (bimabharosaportal.irdai.gov.in) → IGMS complaint
4. Insurance Ombudsman (cioins.co.in) → FREE, within 3 months of rejection, up to ₹50L
5. Consumer Forum (edaakhil.nic.in) → DCDRC up to ₹50L, SCDRC ₹50L-2Cr, NCDRC above ₹2Cr
