COMMON PORTFOLIO PROBLEMS:
- Over-insured in life (multiple endowments) but under-insured in health
- No term insurance — only investment-linked policies with low cover
- Employer group health as only health cover (ends with job change)
- Health SI frozen at purchase level — no inflation adjustment for 5+ years
- No super top-up — one major surgery exhausts entire cover
- Multiple overlapping health policies without understanding contribution clause

COST OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES:
- Replace ₹25L base health with ₹10L base + ₹50L super top-up (saves 20-30%)
- Convert endowment to paid-up + buy term (saves 40-60% premium with higher cover)
- Family floater vs individual: floater cheaper for 2A+2C, but risk of one claim exhausting all
- Long-term premium lock: 2-3 year premium payment saves 5-10%
